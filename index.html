Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app protect my phone?

The app continuously monitors your device for potential risks, including malicious files, unsafe network activity, and system vulnerabilities. It helps prevent unauthorized access, alerts you to suspicious behavior, and provides clear recommendations to keep your phone secure, stable, and running smoothly.

Is the app safe to use?

Yes. The app is designed with security and privacy in mind. It follows industry best practices to protect your data, does not access personal information without permission, and operates transparently to ensure a safe and reliable user experience.

Do I need a subscription to use the app?

The app offers both free and premium features. You can use the core functionality at no cost, while a subscription unlocks advanced tools and additional benefits for enhanced performance and protection.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your device’s app store settings. Once canceled, you will continue to have access to your subscription benefits until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, please contact your device’s app store support Apple App Store and follow their refund process. Refunds are handled directly by the app store according to their policies.
